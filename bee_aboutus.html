- A little about Us -

I share a home with four snails, a Texas eyed click beetle, a gorgeous Spiny Flower Mantis, and more plants than I can count. I love all the amazing creatures in this beautiful world, and I want nothing more than to share that love and infect lots of other people with the plant bug, as I like to call it. I hope my babies make you as happy as they make me. Enjoy!
